# Space Fighter Prototypes

Three gameplay variants exploring different control schemes and enemy types.

## Prototype 1 - Classic Meteor Dodge
- **Controls:** A/D or Arrow Keys (left/right movement)
- **Shooting:** Space or Left Click (manual)
- **Enemies:** Falling meteors with random angles
- **Powerup:** Double shot (temporary, 10 seconds)

## Prototype 2 - 8-Directional Freedom
- **Controls:** WASD or Arrow Keys (8 directions)
- **Shooting:** Space or Left Click (manual)
- **Enemies:** Falling meteors with random angles
- **Powerups:** 4-stage bullet upgrade (permanent) + Invincibility (5 seconds)

## Prototype 3 - Mouse-Controlled Shooter
- **Controls:** Mouse (ship follows cursor)
- **Shooting:** Automatic
- **Enemies:** Alien ships that move side-to-side and shoot back
- **Powerups:** 4-stage bullet upgrade (permanent) + Invincibility (5 seconds)

---

**Quick Switch:** Press 1, 2, or 3 to switch between prototypes
